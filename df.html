CEO & Founder

Chandan Shukla is the visionary founder and CEO of Suresh Infotech Private Limited. With over 15 years of experience in the HR technology sector, he has built the company from the ground up to become one of India's leading recruitment platforms.

Under his leadership, Suresh Infotech has connected over 50,000 job seekers with their ideal careers and helped more than 5,000 companies find exceptional talent.

Chandan holds an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad and is a frequent speaker at industry conferences on the future of work and recruitment technology.

HR Director

Priya Sharma leads the HR operations at Suresh Infotech with over 12 years of experience in talent acquisition and human resource management.

She has developed innovative recruitment strategies that have reduced hiring times by 40% while improving candidate quality. Priya specializes in campus recruitment and has established partnerships with over 50 educational institutions across India.

  • Joint research initiatives in HR technology

Through this partnership, Suresh Infotech gains access to top-tier talent while providing LPU students with exceptional career opportunities and industry exposure.
